Less than a month after sharing her inspiring story, Madeline Stuart has been contacted by more than seven fashion brands as well as multiple make-up and accessory companies who want her to model their products. But the 18-year-old Brisbane teenager, who has Down Syndrome, has not forgotten her real pursuit – to change the stigma around disability - according to her mother Rosanne, who insists that Maddy's life has barely changed since she made international headlines. 'We've had a lot off offers and Maddy is loving it, but it hasn't fazed us much - I've just lost a bit of sleep trying to deal with everything,' Rosanne told Daily Mail Australia.

  • 18-year-old Madeline Stuart, from Brisbane, has Down syndrome
  • Last year she decided to focus on her health and fitness and lost 20 kilos
  • She has been inundated with modeling offers from seven fashion brands
  • Multiple make-up and accessory companies have also contacted the teen
  • She launched her own website and gained over 364K Facebook followers
